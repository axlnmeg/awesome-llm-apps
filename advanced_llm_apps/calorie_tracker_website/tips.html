Tips & Suggestions for a Healthy Lifestyle

Small daily habits that make a big impact on your energy and goals.

Plan Balanced Plates

Aim for half your plate with veggies, one-quarter lean protein, and one-quarter complex carbs.

Smart Snack Strategy

Pair protein with fiber (Greek yogurt + berries) to stabilize blood sugar.

Sleep Consistency

Maintain a consistent bedtime and aim for 7-8 hours to support recovery and metabolism.

Step Goal Momentum

Build toward 7,000-9,000 steps daily by adding 10-minute walks after meals.

Hydration Cue

Keep a 750ml bottle nearby and refill it at least three times daily.

Mindful Portions

Rate your hunger on a 1-10 scale before meals and stop at 7.

Protein at Breakfast

Start the day with 25-30g of protein to manage cravings throughout the day.

Weekly Meal Audit

Review your food log every Sunday to identify wins and gaps.

Recovery Ritual

Schedule one active recovery session each week for mobility and stress reduction.
